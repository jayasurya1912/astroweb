Kandam

Siva Vakkiyar Jothida Nilayam

In Nadi Astrology, the palm leaves are divided into fifteen Kandams (chapters), each revealing unique aspects of the native’s life. The General Kandam gives an introduction with the native’s name, parents, spouse, and an outline of their life. The Education and Wealth Kandam highlights studies, intelligence, financial growth, family happiness, and the power of speech. The Siblings Kandam explains the number of brothers and sisters, their well-being, and relationships.The Mother and Assets Kandam describes the native’s mother, health, house, land, vehicles, and comforts, while the Children Kandam reveals details about progeny, their fortune, and timing of childbirth.
