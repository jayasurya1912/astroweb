AGASTHIYAR NADI

Agasthiyar Nadi is one of the oldest and most revered branches of Nadi astrology, attributed to Sage Agasthiya. These palm leaves contain prophecies and remedies for spiritual upliftment and material life.

The palm leaves of Agasthiyar Nadi contain divine insights about individuals’ destinies, spiritual progress, and remedies for life’s challenges. They are often considered the foundation of Nadi Astrology.

SIVA NADI

Siva Nadi is known for its dialogue format, where predictions are presented as conversations between Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. It is highly revered for its depth and accuracy.

The uniqueness of Siva Nadi lies in its poetic dialogue style. The seeker’s details and predictions are conveyed as if spoken directly by Lord Shiva, offering a divine experience unlike any other.

BOGAR NADI

Bogar Nadi is attributed to the great Siddhar Bogar, renowned for his yogic wisdom and alchemical knowledge. This branch often focuses on spiritual healing and mystical remedies.

The Bogar Nadi palm leaves provide insights into health, medicine, spiritual practices, and remedies. Seekers often consult Bogar Nadi for guidance on both spiritual growth and physical well-being.

KOUSHIKA NADI

Koushika Nadi Astrology is rooted in the wisdom of Sage Koushika, also known as Vishwamitra. This branch is unique for its accuracy in predicting key life events based on thumb impressions.

The Koushika Nadi palm leaves are preserved in Vaitheeswaran Koil, Tamil Nadu. They contain seekers’ names, family details, and predictions, proving this branch to be highly accurate and divine.

VASHISHTA NADI

Vashishta Nadi, attributed to Sage Vashishta, emphasizes spiritual guidance and karma resolution. It helps seekers understand their life path and remedies for past karmas.

Vashishta Nadi is known for detailed life predictions and remedies that align one’s life with dharma. It is often consulted for spiritual clarity and karmic healing.

BHRIGU NADI

Bhrigu Nadi is one of the most widely known branches of Nadi astrology, attributed to Sage Bhrigu. It is renowned for its predictive accuracy and focus on career, wealth, and life purpose.

Bhrigu Nadi offers clear insights into professional life, family, and financial growth. Many seekers rely on Bhrigu Nadi for precise predictions on important life milestones.
